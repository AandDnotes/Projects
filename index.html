Projects

Below are links to a number of my projects, some small and some not so small, some take a little time and then finish and some are ongoing, some require little financial expenditure and some require a lot. A common theme is that they all take me to 'places' I've not been before and so require research, either at the beginning or throughout. Another common theme is that they keep me out of mischief!

The first day of my retirement was Monday 1st July 2013, a month after my 65th birthday, and most of the projects below started after that date. The exception is the Radio Controlled Aircraft project. I joined the Sussex Radio Flying Club in May 2013 in anticipation of retirement.

Where possible I document my endeavours on web pages and these links take you to them.
